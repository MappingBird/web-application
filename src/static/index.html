How It Works

1. Display Bookmarks bar

Click the menu icon at the top-right of your Chrome browser and hover the mouse to Bookmarks. Make sure Show Bookmarks Bar is checked. You can also click Ctrl+Shift+B (or Command+Shit+B on Mac) to show Bookmarks bar.

2. Drag the giant button

Drag "Remember This Place" button to your Bookmarks Bar. Once you see a "+" icon, you can release the mouse button.

3. Select name or address

When you find an interesting place while browsing like the Eiffel Tower, select the "Eiffel Tower" (name of place) text in the web page or the address like "Parc du Champs de Mars, 5 Avenue Anatole France, 75007 Paris, France" (address of the Eiffel Tower) and click the "Remember This Place" button.

4. Save to your map

After clicking the "Remember This Place" button, add comments and unselect images you don't want to save and click "Save to Map."

5. Start your collections

Fantastic! Now you know how to save a place to your collection. You can start collecting the places that you are interested in.
