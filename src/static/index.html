Remember This Place
   

How It Works

1. Display Bookmarks bar

Click the wrench icon at the top-right of your Chrome browser and hover the mouse to Bookmarks. Make sure Show Bookmarks Bar is checked. You can also click Ctrl+Shift+B (or Command+Shit+B on Mac) to show Bookmarks bar.

2. Drag the giant button

Drag "Remember This Place" button to your Bookmarks Bar. Once you see a "+" icon, you can release the mouse.

3. Select name or address

Select "Eiffel Tower" (name of place) or "11 West 53 Street New York, NY 10019, U.S.A." (address of The Museum of Modern Art) and click "Remember This Place" button.

4. Save to your map

After clicked "Remember This Place" button, add commends and deselect irrelative images and click "Save to Map."

5. Start your collections

Fantastic! Now you know how to save a place to your collection. You can start collecting the places where you are interested in.
